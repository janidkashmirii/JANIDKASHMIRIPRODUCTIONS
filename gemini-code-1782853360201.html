About Janid Kashmiri

Hailing from the historic landscape of Srinagar, Kashmir, Janid Kashmiri is an independent multi-disciplinary creator who bridges traditional artistry with digital innovation.

His humble foundations trace back to organic bedroom pop experimentation—penciling verses down on paper while orchestrating rhythm profiles right off his smartphone. That spark resulted in his debut piece, "Umeed".

Today, under Janid Kashmiri Productions, his studio catalogs serve as an intersection where classical cultural poetry meets contemporary ambient design, creating an emotional landscape entirely his own.
